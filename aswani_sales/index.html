Our Story · Since 2000

Supplying retailers, serving customers directly.

Since 2000, Aswani Sales has operated as a footwear wholesale distributor in Narsinghpur, supplying retailers across the region with reliable stock and fair pricing — backed by a retail counter where individual customers can walk in and buy directly.

Our range is led by men's formal and casual footwear, with a thoughtful selection of women's and kids' styles alongside, so retail partners and walk-in shoppers alike find what they need.
